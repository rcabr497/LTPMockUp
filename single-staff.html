    Biography

    Juan M Santos, better known to music fans as "Chulo Jay", is a young Dominican who at an early age has become one of the most renowned DJ in the city of New York. His passion for music started at the age of 3. It was his father and uncle who woke up in Juan the passion for "The Turntables" word used to describe the platform used by DJ's. At the age of 10, he began acting as a DJ in family gatherings and by the age of 16 he owned his own DJ Equipment. His first opportunity to exercise his talents as a professional comes in 2003 when he became the DJ at a local restaurant. That opportunity was the beginning of what would be a journey full of success and recognition. 2004 is a year that marks a definite step in his career. This is the year he decided to create what is today one of the most respected institutions, Los Traficante Productions. Also known as Los Traficante.com. As funder and C.E.O of that institution, he has formed a team of DJ's that have been recognized repeatedly for their support of both local and international talents. Their support has also been recognized by artists who proudly mention his name in their songs. His determination did not stop there, in 2007 he began another facet of his career. "Tipico Y Mas" was the name of the show he host at the time on "Rika FM". His performance in that station is what leads him to create in 2009 his own internet radio station "TraficanteRadio" which immediately becomes a success. His hard work was recognized in 2010 when he received the award for Best DJ in New York from "Los Premios Estrella." The Hard Rock Cafe in Miami, Andy Ranch and Barvaro Beach in the Dominican Republic are some of the venues that make part of an already full agenda of "Chulo Jay" in 2010. "I feel blessed and very fortunate for the achievement I've been able to accomplish so far".
